What's it about?

This is where guitars wail, amps crackle and drummers hit harder than your morning coffee. It’s not just a competition – it’s a rock ‘n roll battlefield where the loudest, most electrifying, emerging rock bands fight for glory.

But here’s the twist: the battle is online. No sweaty crowds – just you deciding the fate of 2025’s hottest up-and-coming talent from you’re the comfort of your screen.

When you hit GO you summon 12 bands selected at random from our arsenal of all the bands playing at independent festivals this year.

They will clash in 3 high-stakes heats, packed with riffs and hooks to shake your speakers and get your neighbours to file for a noise complaint!

Vote for 1 band per heat, then choose which finalist deserves the ultimate bragging rights.
